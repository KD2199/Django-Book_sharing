Digital Platform for Sharing Books

It is well said that sharing gives happiness. Books are form of pure knowledge and help us increase self confidence, boost memory and imagination power.

Book can be the best gift for an individual. Apart from gifting sharing books is also important. In organizations like schools and colleges, this platform will allow the students and faculty members to share and donate their unused or completed books.

The used/unused books, printouts, journals, manuals and every type of study material can be shared via platform provided by us. So, they can be used by the needy instead of being discarded.

The books that are not available in the market or are very expensive, if available with any member can help the other to gain guidance and knowledge. Waste of paper any money for printing study material can be decreased. The inventory of books will increase for the institute though being distributed in nature.
